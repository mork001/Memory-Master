How to Play

  • 1. Click the "Start Game" button to begin.
  • 2. Flip two cards at a time to find matching pairs.
  • 3. Match all pairs to advance to the next level.
  • 4. Earn points and rewards for each match, but watch your moves!
  • 5. If you run out of moves or complete all levels, the game ends.
